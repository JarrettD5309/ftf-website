Sep 5, 2024

Announcing our first release! 34 Trolley is the brainchild of Jarrett Dougherty, drummer of legendary DIY punk band Screaming Females. "Relaxation EP", Jarrett's first solo release, evokes the sound of early 1980s NYC Post Punk Mutant Disco. Think ESG. Think Liquid Liquid. Think Tom Tom Club. The first track, "Coming For You?," is streaming now and pre-orders for the 12" vinyl EP (out October 4) are available at 34trolley.bandcamp.com. The recordings feature Brittany Luna (Catbite) on vocals, Marissa Paternoster (Noun/Screaming Females) and John Hoffman (Vacation) on guitar, and Victor Vieira-Branco (Bark Culture) on vibraphone, amoung others.
